Be Prepared for Opportunity

We advise individuals to contact us the first time they think it is time to consider other opportunities. It is always better to be proactive than reactive. Securing a new position requires addressing important questions such as;

  • Why do I want to make a change?
  • How do personal needs affect your decision making?
  • What do I do well?
  • What are my professional goals?

Kingston Dwight Associates can help you assess your situation and create a plan to evaluate possible opportunities.

  • You just got your CPA designation; is it time to make a change?
  • You know it is time to build out your skill set but you are stuck in the same role?
  • Your prospects for advancement are blurry at best?
